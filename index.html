2. Suspicious App Checker

Found a weird app name in your phone's settings? Type the package name (e.g., com.fake.weather) below to check against our database.

Manual Fix Guide

  1. 1

    Stop The Ads

    Swipe down the notification bar. Turn on Airplane Mode to instantly stop the ads from loading over the internet.

  2. 2

    Find "Blank" Apps

    Go to Settings > Apps. Scroll to the very bottom or top. Look for an app with no name or no icon.

  3. 3

    Force Uninstall

    Tap on that blank app and hit Uninstall. If the button is greyed out, download the PC script on the left to force remove it.
