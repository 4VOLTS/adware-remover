Prerequisites

  1. 1

    Stop The Ads First

    Swipe down your notification bar. Turn on Airplane Mode to stop the ads from loading and interrupting you.

  2. 2

    Unlock Developer Mode

    Go to Settings. Search for "Build Number". Tap it rapidly 7 times until it says you are a developer.

  3. 3

    Enable Debugging

    Search Settings for "USB debugging" and turn it on. Connect the USB cable. Tap Allow on your phone screen when prompted.
