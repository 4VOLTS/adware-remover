Setup Guide

  1. 1

    Enable Airplane Mode

    Swipe down from the top of the screen. Tap the Airplane Mode (✈️) button to temporarily stop ads from popping up.

  2. 2

    Open Settings > Build

    Open the Settings app, search for the word "Build". Tap rapidly 7 times on the Build Number to unlock developer mode.

  3. 3

    Turn On USB Debugging

    In the Settings search, look for "USB debugging". Turn it on, connect the cable to your PC, and tap "Allow" on your phone screen.
